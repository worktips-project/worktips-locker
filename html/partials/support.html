What is Worktips Locker?
Worktips Locker is an open source, re-implementation of https://mymonero.com/. The frontend, html, css, JavaScript, were adapted from, and originally developed by https://mymonero.com/. The Worktips Locker backend is open source, free to use and modify.
What is a Hosted Worktips Account?
The service provides secure, hosted Worktips accounts in order to provide easy access to funds for those that would prefer not to run a full Worktips client themselves, or for those on devices with limited resources. You can access your account on any device, as long as you have access to your private login key. The service never knows your private login key, and is thus unable to spend your funds without your authorisation. All of the private cryptography is done locally on your device.
